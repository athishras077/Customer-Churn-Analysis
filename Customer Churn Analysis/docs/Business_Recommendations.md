# Business Recommendations

# Customer Churn Analysis

---

## Objective

Provide actionable recommendations to reduce customer churn and improve customer retention.

---

## Recommendation 1

### Promote Long-Term Contracts

Encourage customers on month-to-month contracts to switch to one-year or two-year plans by offering discounts and loyalty rewards.

**Expected Benefit**

- Lower churn rate
- Higher customer retention
- Stable recurring revenue

---

## Recommendation 2

### Improve Customer Onboarding

Customers with low tenure have the highest churn.

Provide:

- Welcome offers
- Product education
- Regular follow-ups
- Personalized support

**Expected Benefit**

- Improved customer engagement
- Better retention during the first year

---

## Recommendation 3

### Improve Customer Satisfaction

Monitor satisfaction scores regularly and proactively contact dissatisfied customers.

**Expected Benefit**

- Increased customer loyalty
- Lower churn rate

---

## Recommendation 4

### Review Pricing Strategy

Evaluate pricing plans for customers with high monthly charges.

Consider:

- Bundled services
- Flexible pricing
- Promotional discounts

**Expected Benefit**

- Improved customer value perception
- Reduced churn

---

## Recommendation 5

### Strengthen Customer Support

Improve technical support and customer service response times.

Provide:

- Faster issue resolution
- Better technical assistance
- Improved online support

**Expected Benefit**

- Higher customer satisfaction
- Increased retention

---

## Recommendation 6

### Retain High-Value Customers

Identify customers with high CLTV and provide:

- Loyalty rewards
- Exclusive offers
- Personalized communication

**Expected Benefit**

- Protect revenue
- Increase customer lifetime value

---

## Recommendation 7

### Use Data-Driven Monitoring

Monitor customer churn KPIs through the Power BI dashboard.

Track:

- Churn Rate
- Revenue Lost
- Customer Satisfaction
- Contract Distribution
- Customer Tenure

**Expected Benefit**

- Faster business decisions
- Early identification of churn risks

---

## Conclusion

Implementing these recommendations can help reduce customer churn, improve customer satisfaction, increase customer lifetime value, and support sustainable business growth.
