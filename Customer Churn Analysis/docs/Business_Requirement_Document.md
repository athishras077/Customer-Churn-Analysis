# Business Requirement Document (BRD)

# Customer Churn Analysis

---

## 1. Business Problem Statement

The telecommunications company is experiencing customer churn, which is affecting revenue and business growth. The company wants to identify the reasons why customers leave and find ways to improve customer retention.

---

## 2. Project Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze customer data to identify the key factors contributing to customer churn and provide data-driven recommendations to reduce churn and improve customer retention.

---

## 3. Business Scenario

The management team has noticed that many customers are canceling their services. They have asked the Data Analytics team to analyze historical customer data and answer important business questions related to customer churn.

As a Data Analyst, my responsibility is to analyze the data, discover patterns, build dashboards, and provide actionable business insights.

---

## 4. Stakeholders

- CEO
- Sales Team
- Marketing Team
- Customer Success Team
- Product Team
- Finance Team

---

## 5. Business Goals

- Measure the overall customer churn rate.
- Identify the factors influencing customer churn.
- Analyze customer demographics and service usage.
- Discover high-risk customer segments.
- Provide recommendations to reduce customer churn.
- Create an interactive Power BI dashboard for business users.

---

## 6. Business Questions

The project aims to answer the following questions:

- What is the overall customer churn rate?
- Which customer groups have the highest churn?
- Does contract type affect customer churn?
- Does tenure influence churn?
- Which payment methods have the highest churn?
- Does internet service type affect churn?
- Which services help improve customer retention?
- How do monthly charges impact churn?
- What business actions can help reduce customer churn?

---

## 7. Project Scope

### In Scope

- Customer demographic analysis
- Service usage analysis
- Contract analysis
- Payment analysis
- Revenue analysis
- Customer churn analysis
- Dashboard creation
- Business recommendations

### Out of Scope

- Machine Learning prediction models
- Customer Lifetime Value prediction
- Real-time customer monitoring
- Marketing campaign implementation

---

## 8. Expected Outcomes

- Understand the main reasons for customer churn.
- Identify high-risk customer segments.
- Measure important business KPIs.
- Build an interactive Power BI dashboard.
- Provide data-driven recommendations for reducing customer churn.

---

## 9. Success Metrics

The project will be considered successful if it can:

- Calculate the overall churn rate.
- Identify the major factors affecting churn.
- Segment customers based on churn behavior.
- Present insights through an interactive dashboard.
- Deliver practical business recommendations supported by data.
