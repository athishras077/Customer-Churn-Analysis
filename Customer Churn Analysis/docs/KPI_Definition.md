# KPI Definition

# Customer Churn Analysis

---

## Objective

The objective of this phase is to define the Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) that will measure customer churn, customer retention, revenue, and business performance. These KPIs will be used in the Power BI dashboard to support business decision-making.

---

## Customer KPIs

- Total Customers
- Churned Customers
- Active Customers
- Churn Rate
- Customer Retention Rate

---

## Revenue KPIs

- Total Revenue
- Revenue Lost Due to Churn
- Average Monthly Charge
- Average Revenue per Customer

---

## Service KPIs

- Average Tenure
- Internet Service Distribution
- Contract Distribution

---

## Customer Experience KPIs

- Average Satisfaction Score
- Average Churn Score
- Average Customer Lifetime Value (CLTV)

---

## Dashboard KPI Cards

- Total Customers
- Churned Customers
- Churn Rate
- Retention Rate
- Total Revenue
- Revenue Lost
- Average Monthly Charge
- Average Tenure
- Average Satisfaction Score
- Average CLTV

---

## Conclusion

These KPIs provide a comprehensive view of customer behavior, churn trends, revenue performance, and customer satisfaction. They form the foundation of the Power BI dashboard and support data-driven business decisions.
