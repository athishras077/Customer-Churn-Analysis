# Dashboard Design Plan

# Customer Churn Analysis

---

## Objective

Design an interactive Power BI dashboard that enables business stakeholders to monitor customer churn, customer retention, revenue performance, and customer behavior through clear and actionable visualizations.

---

# Dashboard Structure

## Page 1 – Executive Overview

### KPI Cards

- Total Customers
- Churned Customers
- Churn Rate
- Retention Rate
- Total Revenue
- Revenue Lost

### Visuals

- Churn Distribution
- Contract vs Churn
- Internet Type vs Churn
- Payment Method vs Churn

### Slicers

- Gender
- Contract
- Internet Type
- Customer Status

---

## Page 2 – Customer & Service Analysis

### Visuals

- Churn by Gender
- Churn by Senior Citizen
- Monthly Charge vs Churn
- Tenure vs Churn
- Satisfaction Score vs Churn
- Customer Status by Contract

### Slicers

- Married
- Dependents
- Payment Method

---

## Page 3 – Revenue & Business Insights

### KPI Cards

- Average Monthly Charge
- Average Tenure
- Average Satisfaction Score
- Average CLTV

### Visuals

- Revenue by Customer Status
- Top 10 Customers by Revenue
- Contract × Internet Type Matrix
- Business Insights Table
- Business Recommendations

---

## Dashboard Features

- Interactive slicers
- KPI cards
- Consistent color theme
- Page navigation
- Executive-friendly layout

---

## Conclusion

The dashboard is designed to provide executives and managers with a clear view of customer churn, revenue performance, customer behavior, and actionable business insights. The planned layout supports fast decision-making through interactive and easy-to-understand visualizations.
