# Executive Summary

# Customer Churn Analysis

---

## Project Overview

The objective of this project was to analyze customer churn using historical customer data and identify the key factors affecting customer retention. The project followed the complete industry-standard data analysis lifecycle, including business understanding, data cleaning, exploratory data analysis, SQL analysis, KPI development, dashboard creation, and business recommendations.

- Business Insights
- Business Recommendations

---

## Key Findings

- Customer churn is strongly influenced by contract type.
- Customers with shorter tenure are more likely to churn.
- Higher monthly charges are associated with increased churn.
- Lower customer satisfaction contributes to higher churn.
- High-value customers contribute significantly to overall revenue.

---

## Business Outcome

The project provides management with valuable insights into customer behavior and highlights opportunities to improve customer retention through targeted strategies, improved customer service, pricing optimization, and loyalty programs.
